Pre-Close Checklist
  • All GRNs received and posted

What Locking Does
  • Prevents new Purchase Orders in period
  • Blocks GRN / Supplier Invoice creation
  • Blocks Debit Note posting
  • Only Post-permission users can lock/unlock
FX Revaluation Notes
  • Run once per period at period-end date
  • Affects all open foreign-currency AP
  • Posts to Unrealized FX Gain/Loss ledger
  • Reversed automatically on next open period
